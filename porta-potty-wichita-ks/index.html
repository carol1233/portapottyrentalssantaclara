Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle emergency deliveries?

Yes, we assist with urgent requests when inventory allows.

Do you charge extra for same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available based on unit availability and location. In most cases, an additional fee may apply, but we’ll always confirm the cost upfront before dispatching.

How many porta-potties do I need for my site?

Usage rates, foot traffic, and shift schedules determine the recommended quantity.

Can the towable porta-potties handle rough terrain?

Yes. They’re mounted on trailers with heavy-duty off-road tires and built with rugged polyethylene construction, so they can move across farms, construction sites, or event grounds with ease.

Do your restroom trailers stay cool during summer when it gets hot?

Yes. Our restroom trailers are climate-controlled with both heating and air conditioning, so your guests stay comfortable whether it’s summer heat or winter chill.

Can I extend my rental period if my project or event runs longer than expected?

Yes! You can extend your rental by reaching out to our customer service team.

Wichita, KS Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Wichita (/ˈwɪtʃɪtɔː/ WITCH-ih-taw) is the most populous city in the U.S. state of Kansas and the county seat of Sedgwick County. As of the 2020 census, the population of the city was 397,532. The Wichita metro area had a population of 647,610 in 2020. It is located in south-central Kansas on the Arkansas River.
